Initial Meeting

You will meet with one of our buyer's representatives. They will serve you throughout the build process from initial contact to the closing date. They are here to ensure that all of your needs are met. They will give you an orientation to the company and it's values. They will walk you through all the steps and answer any questions.
Pre-qualify

In order for us to proceed with any additional steps we will need you to pre-qualify. Pre-qualification is important since it determines which floor plans and options that you can select. You can choose to pre-qualify with our lenders (which come with perks) or with you preferred lender.
Select Options

This is the funniest part of the whole process. You buyer's representative will show you options that you can customize your chosen floor plan with! They will set you up with a consultation appointment with our options' outpost. Here you can touch and see all the available options for your home ranging from flooring options to light fixtures.
