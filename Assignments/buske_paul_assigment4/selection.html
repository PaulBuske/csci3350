Selection

The selection lesson focused on using Photoshop tools that select and move the selection to another position in Photoshop. The selection tools that were used were quick selection, the epithelial marquee, the rectangle selection, the magic wand, the lasso, and the magnetic lasso. Then I used the elliptical section tool to duplicate the screws and rotate them. I cropped out the original location of the moved elements once the screws were in place.
