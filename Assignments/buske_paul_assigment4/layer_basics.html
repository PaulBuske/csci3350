Layer Basics

The layer basics lesson covered how to transfer layers between Photoshop files, rearrange the layers, edit the layers, and add a background layer using a filter tab. We duplicated the pineapple layer and then used overlay blending to make the pineapple more vibrant. We took the beach from another photo and then transformed and edited it to look like the beach photo under the flower. Then we reduced the the opacity of the postage stamp layer to make it more transparent. Then we added a flower image from another photo. The last touch we applied were to add a drop shadow to the "Island Paradise" and then create the Hawaii text with a green stoke and a yellow gradient. I used the eyedropper tool to sample the grass on the beach to make the green in the Hawaii stroke and the "Island Paradise".
